Just Released

My newest book is Proust's Duchess: How Three Celebrated Women Captured the Imagination of Fin-de-Siècle Paris, described by Kirkus as "a captivating triple biography [of the] women who inspired Marcel Proust's Duchesse de Guermantes..., a palpable, engrossing portrait of three extraordinary women and their tempestuous, fragile world" (starred review). Focused on the interplay between aristocracy and artistic genius, the book shows how its heroines, the reigning queens of late-19th-century Parisian society, turned themselves into living legends, becoming famous for being famous, and so pioneering today's ubiquitous cult of celebrity.
